Moab Hiking Guide

Selection Criteria

  • Drive Time: No more than 45 minutes from Moab, Utah
  • Duration: 2 to 4 hours per hike
  • Variety: Each hike offers a distinct terrain or experience
  • Crowds: Avoids car lines or long entry waits when possible
